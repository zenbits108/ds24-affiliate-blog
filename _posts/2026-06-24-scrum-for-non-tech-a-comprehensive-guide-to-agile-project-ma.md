"Scrum for Non-Tech" is ideal for anyone looking to improve their project management skills in a non-technical setting. Here are some specific groups who would benefit from this course:

### Project Managers

### Team Leaders
As a team leader in any department—marketing, finance, HR—you might find it challenging to keep track of multiple tasks and deadlines. Scrum provides tools and techniques to streamline your workflow and ensure everyone is aligned towards common goals.
